‚ùì Help & Support

How do I book a shuttle?

Click the "Request Ride" button on your dashboard, select your pickup and destination, then submit your request.

How can I track my shuttle?

Use the "Track Shuttle" button or open the live map to see real-time shuttle locations.

What should I do in an emergency?

Use the Emergency Alert button or call campus security at the emergency number provided.

How do I cancel a ride?

Go to "My Rides" and click the cancel button on your upcoming ride.
